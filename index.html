Why Financial Literacy Should Be Taught in Schools


Financial Literacy is the understanding of how to earn, manage, and invent money.
Many people struggle with financial literacy, often because of the lack of understanding.
Financial literacy is important as it helps people to make informed decisions with their money.
By teaching finanical literacy in schools, students can gain the essential skills, such as budgeting, saving, and understanding credit.


Key Benefits of Financial Lieracy Education

  1. Helps students manage personal finances
  2. Encourages saving money
  3. Promotes understanding and critical thinking
  4. Prepares for financial responsibilities
  5. Increase confidence in handling finances
